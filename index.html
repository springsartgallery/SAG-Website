SPRINGS ART GALLERY

OPEN CALL FOR ART EXHIBITIONS IN 2026/2027

THE VISION

The Springs Art Gallery is the only civic gallery in the City of Ekurhuleni. We especially welcome proposals from members of marginalised communities in South Africa, whose voices are often ignored in traditional arts establishments. We provide a space to share messages and help transform Springs into a more inclusive destination.

The Venue

An 80sq-m exhibition space offering both internal and external exhibition models. We showcase emerging artists alongside national established artists across all media.

What We Look For

Art that reflects our community and the vast world beyond; art that presents new ideas, deconstructs traditional forms, and encourages creativity.

PROGRAMME SUPPORT

We Provide

  • • Free Exhibition Venue
  • • Professional Installation Support
  • • Marketing & PR Strategy
  • • Liability Coverage/Insurance
  • • Artistic Professional Guidance

Sales of Work

SAG does not charge the artist any commission. Artists retain full control over pricing, and all sales arrangements are handled by artists themselves.
