CULTIVATING CREATIVITY IN EKURHULENI

The Springs Art Gallery (SAG) is the only civic gallery in the City of Ekurhuleni. Our mission for the 2026/2027 cycle is to bridge the gap between emerging talent and professional art markets through experimental exhibition series.

We invite proposals for our External Exhibition Programme, where artists organize their own shows, book the 80sq-m space for 1-3 months, and retain 100% of their sales.

The Venue

Located in the Library Building, Springs CBD. Open Mon-Fri 09h00–16h00, Sat 08h00–12h00. Admission is free to the public.

The Support

Successful applicants receive free venue use, professional installation support, marketing strategy, and liability coverage.

The Submission

Window opens January 5, 2026. Closes March 16, 2026. Notifications will be finalized by end-March 2026.
